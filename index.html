PogoTeams

PvP Team Builder for Pokémon GO Battle League

PogoTeams is a competitive planning tool for Pokémon GO Battle League. Build PvP teams using established team-building principles and matchup logic.

Features

  • Automatic team building – Generate competitive teams using structured team-building rules
  • Lead gameplans – Get structured lead guidance for any team
  • Analysis tools – Evaluate coverage, consistency, bulk, and safety
  • Matchup breakdowns – Analyze matchups against popular Pokémon and recorded opponent teams
