Lead Gameplans

Plan your opening matchups using structured decision rules—no more guessing what to do at the start of a battle.

  • Know when to stay in a matchup or switch out

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes! Free to use with ads. You can remove them permanently with a small one-time purchase.

Is the app available on Android?

Yes! Available on iOS, Mac, and Android. Download it from the App Store or Google Play.

Can I import Pokémon directly from the game?

No — for your account's safety. Pokémon GO's API is protected and third-party connections could result in a ban.

Do I have to enter my Pokémon every time?

No. You select them once and they're saved across all leagues and cups.

What is "Lead Gameplan"?

For every saved team, you can view short, matchup-specific guides on how to play your lead against common opposing Pokémon.
