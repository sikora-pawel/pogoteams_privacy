Build PvP teams using structured team-building rules from high-level play. Plan your leads with matchup logic, not guesswork. Climb the ranks with a real competitive edge.

Features

Tools built for competitive trainers

Apply proven team-building concepts and structured decision rules used in high-level Pokémon GO PvP.

Automatic Team Building

Generate teams using structured rules commonly applied in high-level PvP—role balance, coverage, and safe switches.

  • Build teams from the Pokémon you own
  • Build around any Pokémon and get a team that works
  • Use recorded opponents to detect common picks and cores
  • Generate teams designed to answer the meta you encounter

Lead Gameplans

Plan your opening matchups using structured decision rules—no more guessing what to do at the start of a battle.

  • Know when to stay in a matchup or switch out
  • Learn when to commit shields and when to preserve them
  • Get catch and bait suggestions where they matter
  • See how matchups change across different shield scenarios

Analysis & Planning Tools

Evaluate teams using well-known PvP metrics—coverage, consistency, bulk, and safety.

  • Pokémon rankings by cup and role, designed for PvP
  • Matchup breakdowns vs popular picks and recorded opponents
  • Identify the most dangerous matchups for each team
  • Guided manual team builder with rule-based suggestions
