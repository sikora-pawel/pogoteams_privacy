Niantic just announced GBL Season 26 — **Memories in Motion** — starting March 3, 2026. PoGoTeams already has early rankings and cup data available so you can start building teams before the season goes live.

The data is based on what's been announced so far. Some details may still change before the season starts, but there's enough to start planning.

## How to access the preview

1. Open **Settings** (gear icon in the top right)
2. Find the **Season Preview** toggle at the top
3. Turn it on

![Season Preview toggle in Settings](/assets/blog/season-preview-settings.jpg)

The app will reload with Memories in Motion data — updated rankings, new cup formats, and move changes all reflected in team building, matchup analysis, and lead gameplans.

When you're done exploring, toggle it off to go back to the current season's data. Both datasets are cached separately, so switching is quick.

## What's changing in Memories in Motion

The season brings some notable move rebalances and new cup formats:

- **Drain Punch** power doubled (20 → 40)
- **Sand Tomb** power increased (25 → 40)
- **Twister** reworked (45 → 70)
- **Low Kick** buffed (4 → 5)
- **Heat Wave** nerfed (95 → 75)
- Several Pokémon gaining new moves — Gliscor, Moltres, Heracross, Garchomp, and more

Cup rotation includes Kanto Cup, Spring Cup, Jungle Cup, Electric Cup, Fantasy Cup, and Catch Cup alongside the standard Great/Ultra/Master League formats.

Full details on the [official announcement](https://pokemongo.com/news/go-battle-league-memories-in-motion).

## Keep in mind

This is preview data. Rankings are generated from what's been announced and may shift as more details are confirmed. We'll keep updating the data as things get finalized — just toggle the preview again to pick up changes.

Season preview is available now on iOS and Android.
